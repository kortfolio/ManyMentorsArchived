Join the Mentoring movement!

Mentoring in the STEM fields is increasingly being recognized as a vital strategy to excite the next generation about the innovation, creativity, and problem-solving strategies that are at the heart of work in STEM.

Be a leader and start a chapter to bring STEM mentoring to your city and positively impact the lives of countless youth.

Requirements

  • Must be 18 years or older
  • Must pass background check
  • Must commit to at least 1 academic year of leadership
  • Must recruit at least 2 other leadership team members
    •

Getting started

1. Complete our application form to indicate your interest in starting a chapter.

2. Participate in an introductory call so we can get to know you and answer your questions.

3. Complete any necessary paperwork to formally establish a chapter.

4. With our help, develop a strategic plan to recruit, train, and launch an online and in-person mentoring program with the partner(s) of your choice.

What to expect

  • Our complete SUPPORT!! Your success as a chapter is directly linked to the fulfillment of our mission. We will be there for guidance every step of the way.
  • Virtual training, event guides and monthly ideas for programming.
  • Promotion of your chapter through our social media outlets and monthly newsletter.
  • Access to our leadership network to gain support from other chapters who will share insight, guidance, and expertise.

    • what you will gain

  • Personal fulfillment If you are passionate about guiding the next generation towards opportunity in STEM, we provide the infrastructure to do so.
  • Professional development You will develop invaluable skills in leadership, networking, presenting, strategic planning, and more.
  • Access Mentors in our organization span across the country and range from college students to scientist and engineers in national labs. As a leadership member, you will have exclusive access to these networks.
  • Visibility Distinguish yourself by leading cutting edge STEM mentoring efforts to meet the national needs we have for innovators in these fields.
